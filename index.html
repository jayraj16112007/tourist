Culture of Gujarat

Garba & Dandiya

Gujarat's iconic folk dances, Garba and Dandiya, are vibrant performances celebrated during Navratri across the state.

Gujarati Dish

Traditional food includes dhokla, thepla, khichdi, and farsan served in the famous Gujarati thali, blending sweet, sour, and spicy flavors.

Festivals

Festivals like Navratri, Uttarayan, and Rann Utsav reflect Gujarat's deep-rooted traditions, colorful attire, and joy-filled spirit.

Traditional Dress

Men wear kediyu and dhoti; women wear chaniya choli with intricate mirror work, showcasing Gujarat's textile legacy like Bandhani and Patola.
